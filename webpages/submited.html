How to activate your camera

  1. Copy into your browser's navigation bar chrome://flags/#unsafely-treat-insecure-origin-as-secure and press the enter key
  2. Copy into the text box the URL of the platform you are going to use NAVI Assistant with. For example, if you are going to use AppInventor, you must copy inside the text box http://ai2.appinventor.mit.edu and in the right button select Enabled
  3. That's all! Don't forget when Appinventor will be available to give permissions for the camera to record
